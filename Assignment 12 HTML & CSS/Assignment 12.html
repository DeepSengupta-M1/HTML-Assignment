    • Shiksha 'O' Anushandhan (SOA), formerly Shiksha 'O' Anushandhan University (SOA University), is a private deemed University located in Bhubaneswar , Odisha, India.[2]The University is composed of nine degree-granting schools and colleges and has a student body of around 15,000. Many of SOA's programs are nationally accredited, including engineering, medicine, pharmacy, business, nursing, biotechnology, science, humanities, enviroment, nano technology, materials science, agriculture and law.

    It was founded as Institute of Technical Education and Research(ITER) in 1996 at Bhubaneswar and it received AICTE approval for B.Tech. Since its inception it was affilated to Utkal University. Subsequently, it was affilated to Biju Patnaik University of Technology(BPUT) between 2002 and 2007. Recognizing its contribution to technical and professional education the University Grants Commission(UGC), in 2007, declared Siksha 'O' Anushandhanas as a Deemed to be University by the U/S 3 of the UGC Act, 1956. In 2007, the Institute of Technical Education and Research(ITER), Bhubaneswar became a constituent institute of Siksha 'O' Anushandhan University. Institutes, schools and departments of SOA University are spread over 200 acres (0.81 km2) campuses in the city of Bhubaneswar. SOA has been granted Category-II Graded Autonomy Status by University Grants Commission in 2018.[3]

    Siksha 'O' Anushandhan(SOA), located in the city of Bhubaneswar, Odisha, India is presently one amongst the leading higher education institutions in the country. It is Deemed to be University declared under section 3 of the University Grants Commission Act., 1956, India.

    It is actively engaged in education and research in Engineering & Technology, Medical & Dental Sciences, Pharmaceutical Sciences, Biotechnology, Management Sciences, Nursing, Hospitality & Tourism Management, Legal Studies, Agricultural Sciences, Basic Sciences & Humanities at Undergraduate, Post-Graduate, Post-Master's in Medical Sciences, Doctoral and Post-Doctoral level. All the programmes have been approved and recognized by respective statutory councils of India.
